About Me

Noushin Syeara Rodoshi is a Civil Engineering graduate from the Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) , where she majored in Transportation Engineering and completed her B.Sc. with a strong academic record.

Her primary interests lie in intelligent transport systems, transportation system modelling, and sustainable transportation and urban planning. She has worked on freight generation and attraction modelling for Dhaka City, focusing on establishment-level freight trip attraction and data-driven methods for understanding urban freight patterns.

Noushin has contributed to projects spanning computer vision and machine learning for transportation applications, including YOLOv8-based multimodal traffic detection for Dhaka, video-based traffic counts, and accident severity prediction using classical and ensemble learning techniques. These projects reflect a blend of transportation engineering, data analytics, and practical problem solving.

Beyond academics and research, she enjoys building communities and experiences. She is a founding member of the BUET Innovation and Development Club (BIDC), has performed as a vocalist in the band “Bihongo,” and has helped organize major departmental events such as BUET CE Fest 2023 and Civil Day 2024.
